Research Portfolio

Interactions between motor behavior & episodic memory

Series of novel online experiments revealing that engaging in well-learned actions during learning improves long-term memory.

key tools & methods: R, statistical analysis, data visualization, online experimentation, dashboarding

Exposure to novelty in everyday life boots long-term memory

Intensive longitudinal survey study demonstrating that doing new things as part of everyday life — going to new places, talking to new people — has strong and widespread benefits for autobiographical memory.

key tools & methods: R, Qualtrics, survey design, statistical analysis, data visualization, longitudinal research
